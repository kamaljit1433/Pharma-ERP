# Employee Management System (EMS)

## Product Overview

The Employee Management System is a comprehensive, web-based Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to manage the complete employee lifecycle — from recruitment and onboarding through to offboarding and separation.

## Core Purpose

Automate repetitive HR processes, ensure accurate payroll based on real attendance data, provide employees with a self-service portal, and enable management with real-time visibility into workforce operations.

## Key Modules

### Core Modules
- **Employee Information Management** - Centralized employee records with personal, employment, and document history
- **Recruitment & Onboarding** - End-to-end hiring process from job posting through onboarding checklists
- **Attendance & Time Management** - Multiple attendance marking modes (biometric, GPS, web portal) with shift management
- **Leave Management** - Leave request workflows with balance tracking and approval routing
- **Payroll Management** - Automated salary calculation based on attendance with statutory deductions
- **Benefits & Compensation** - Health insurance, PF, gratuity, reimbursements, and rewards management
- **Separation & Offboarding** - Exit process management with full & final settlement and asset recovery
- **Performance Management** - OKR/KPI goal setting, performance reviews, and continuous feedback
- **Training & Certification** - Training program enrollment, certification tracking, and skill matrix management

### Extended Features
- **Face Recognition / Biometric Attendance** - Human presence detection (liveness detection, no facial identity matching)
- **Geo Tracking & Travel Allowance** - GPS-based travel tracking with automatic allowance calculation
- **Company Hierarchy System** - Organizational structure with reporting lines and org chart visualization
- **Supplier & Buyer Management** - Employee-managed supplier/buyer records with visit tracking
- **Attendance-Based Salary Calculation** - Payroll directly tied to actual attendance records
- **Check-In / Check-Out & Leave System** - Digital attendance marking with integrated leave management
- **Bank Details Management** - Secure employee bank account storage for salary disbursement
- **Salary Calculation Engine** - Flexible salary modes (monthly fixed, daily rate, hourly rate, half-day)

## User Roles

- **Super Admin** - Full system access and configuration
- **HR Manager** - Employee records, payroll, leaves, documents, e-signature
- **Department Manager** - Team attendance, approvals, hierarchy view
- **Finance / Payroll** - Salary, bank details, travel allowance, expense claims
- **Employee** - Self-service portal for attendance, leave, documents, bank details
- **IT Admin** - System settings, device management, access control

## Key Characteristics

- **Progressive Web App** - Works on desktop and mobile, installable, offline-capable
- **Real-time Visibility** - Live attendance, leave, and payroll dashboards
- **Automated Workflows** - Leave approvals, payroll processing, notifications
- **Compliance-Ready** - Audit logs, statutory deductions, document management
- **Scalable** - Supports unlimited employees and organizational complexity
